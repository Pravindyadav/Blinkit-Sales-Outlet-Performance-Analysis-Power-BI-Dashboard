# 🛒 Blinkit Sales & Outlet Performance Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze Blinkit's sales performance, customer ratings, inventory distribution, and outlet performance.

The dashboard provides actionable insights into sales trends, outlet efficiency, product category performance, and customer satisfaction.

## 🎯 Business Objective

To conduct a comprehensive analysis of Blinkit's sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution to identify opportunities for optimization and business growth.

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Sales: $1.2M
- Average Sales: $141
- Number of Items Sold: 8,523
- Average Customer Rating: 3.9

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1. Total Sales by Fat Content
- Analyze impact of fat content on sales.
- Compare Low Fat vs Regular products.

### 2. Total Sales by Item Type
- Identify best-performing product categories.

### 3. Fat Content by Outlet
- Compare sales across outlet tiers.

### 4. Outlet Establishment Analysis
- Understand sales performance by establishment year.

### 5. Sales by Outlet Size
- Analyze revenue contribution from different outlet sizes.

### 6. Sales by Outlet Location
- Compare sales across Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3 locations.

### 7. Outlet Type Analysis
- Evaluate outlet performance using:
  - Total Sales
  - Number of Items
  - Average Sales
  - Average Rating

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Generated over $1.2M in total sales.
- Tier 3 outlets contributed the highest revenue.
- Medium-sized outlets showed the strongest sales performance.
- Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods emerged as top-selling categories.
- Supermarket Type 1 generated the highest sales among outlet types.
